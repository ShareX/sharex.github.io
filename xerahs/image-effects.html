Image Effects

XerahS includes a powerful image processing engine that allows you to apply a wide range of effects to your screenshots automatically after capture.

Effects Library

Effects are categorized into three main groups:

Manipulations

  • Resize: Scale images by percentage or specific dimensions.
  • Rotate: Rotate by 90, 180, 270 degrees or custom angles.
  • Flip: Flip horizontally or vertically.
  • Skew: Apply perspective skewing.
  • Auto Crop: Automatically remove transparent or solid color borders.
  • Rounded Corners: Add rounded corners with customizable radius.
  • Rotate 3D: Apply 3D rotation transformations.

Adjustments

  • Brightness/Contrast: Adjust luminance and tonal range.
  • Gamma: Correct gamma levels.
  • Hue/Saturation: Shift colors and adjust intensity.
  • Grayscale: Convert to black and white.
  • Sepia: Apply a vintage tone.
  • Invert: Invert colors.
  • Colorize: Tint the image with a specific color.
  • Replace Color: Swap one color for another.

Filters

  • Blur: Gaussian blur for privacy or aesthetics.
  • Pixelate: Obfuscate sensitive information.
  • Sharpen: Enhance edge detail.
  • Border: Add solid or gradient borders.
  • Shadow: Add drop shadows.
  • Reflection: Create a mirrored reflection.
  • Glow: Add an outer glow effect.
  • Torn Edge: Simulate a torn paper edge.

Import / Export

XerahS allows you to share your custom effect configurations or back them up.

Legacy Support: XerahS includes a legacy importer that can read existing ShareX image effect files (.sxie), allowing for a seamless migration of your favorite presets.
  • Export: Save your current effect chain to a JSON-based format for sharing.
  • Import: Load presets created by the community or from previous backups.
